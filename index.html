Student-Facing Dashboard

Compare college majors by pay and in-person career pathways.

This view combines Census PSEO earnings and employment-flow data with BLS telework rates to estimate how in-person the jobs tied to each major tend to be five years after graduation.

Index logic

In-personness = weighted average of industry in-person shares

Higher values mean the major more often feeds into industries with less telework.
